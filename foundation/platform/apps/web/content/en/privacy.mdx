# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

## 1. Data Controller

The data controller is **Certo Governance Institute** with headquarters in Poland.
Contact: [privacy@certogov.org](mailto:privacy@certogov.org)

## 2. What data we collect

- **Authentication data:** email address, name and surname, profile picture — collected during login via Google or Microsoft OAuth.
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, visit time — automatically collected in server logs.
- **Session data:** authentication tokens stored in cookies.

## 3. Purpose and legal basis for processing

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---------|------------|
| Enabling login and access to documents | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR |
| Ensuring service security | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR |
| Conducting statistics | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR |
| Fulfilling legal obligations | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR |

## 4. Data Recipients

- **Supabase Inc.** — database and authentication infrastructure.
- **Vercel Inc.** — hosting infrastructure.
- **Google LLC** — Google OAuth login.
- **Microsoft Corporation** — Microsoft OAuth login.

## 5. User Rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection.
To exercise your rights: [privacy@certogov.org](mailto:privacy@certogov.org).
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the President of UODO.

## 6. Cookies

The service uses only session and authentication cookies. We do not use marketing or tracking cookies.

## 7. Data Retention Period

Authentication data — for the duration of account ownership. Technical logs — 90 days. Upon account deletion, data is anonymized within 30 days.
