# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

## 1. Data Controller

The data controller is **Certo Governance Institute** with registered office in Poland.
Contact: [privacy@certogov.org](mailto:privacy@certogov.org)

## 2. What data we collect

- **Authentication data:** email address, first and last name, profile picture — obtained during login via Google or Microsoft OAuth.
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, visit time — collected automatically in server logs.
- **Session data:** authentication tokens stored in cookies.

## 3. Purpose and legal basis for processing

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---------|------------|
| Enable login and access to documents | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR |
| Ensure service security | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR |
| Conduct statistics | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR |
| Fulfil legal obligations | Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR |

## 4. Data recipients

- **Supabase Inc.** — database infrastructure and authentication.
- **Vercel Inc.** — hosting infrastructure.
- **Google LLC** — Google OAuth login.
- **Microsoft Corporation** — Microsoft OAuth login.

## 5. User rights

You have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection.
To exercise your rights: [privacy@certogov.org](mailto:privacy@certogov.org).
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the President of UODO.

## 6. Cookies

The service uses only session and authentication cookies. We do not use marketing or tracking cookies.

## 7. Retention period

Authentication data — for the duration of account ownership. Technical logs — 90 days. After account deletion, data is anonymised within 30 days.
