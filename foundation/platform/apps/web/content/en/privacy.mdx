# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

## 1. Data Controller

The data controller is **Certo Governance Institute** with its registered office in Poland.
Contact: [privacy@certogov.org](mailto:privacy@certogov.org)

## 2. What data we collect

- **Authentication data:** email address, full name, profile picture — obtained during login via Google or Microsoft OAuth.
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, visit time — automatically collected in server logs.
- **Session data:** authentication tokens stored in cookies.

## 3. Purpose and legal basis for processing

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---------|------------|
| Enabling login and access to documents | Article 6(1)(b) GDPR |
| Ensuring service security | Article 6(1)(f) GDPR |
| Conducting statistics | Article 6(1)(f) GDPR |
| Fulfilling legal obligations | Article 6(1)(c) GDPR |

## 4. Data recipients

- **Supabase Inc.** — database infrastructure and authentication.
- **Vercel Inc.** — hosting infrastructure.
- **Google LLC** — login via Google OAuth.
- **Microsoft Corporation** — login via Microsoft OAuth.

## 5. User rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing.
To exercise your rights: [privacy@certogov.org](mailto:privacy@certogov.org).
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the President of the UODO.

## 6. Cookies

The service uses cookies exclusively for session and authentication purposes. We do not use marketing or tracking cookies.

## 7. Retention period

Authentication data — for the duration of account ownership. Technical logs — 90 days. Following account deletion, data is anonymized within 30 days.
