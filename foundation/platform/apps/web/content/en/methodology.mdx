## Certo Rating Index

Certo develops and publishes a periodic, comparative index of public credibility based on governance quality — the Certo Rating Index, covering entities, sectors or countries subject to the Foundation's evaluation activities. The Index is based on aggregated rating results from the Foundation, data on the dynamics of governance quality changes over time, and publicly available comparative data. Each edition of the Index contains a forecasting component indicating expected directions of change based on declared development objectives of assessed entities and identified trends.

## What is Certo Rating

Certo Rating is not an opinion. It is a cryptographic artifact of the process — a record of a decision that exists independently of its creator's infrastructure. Each rating is:

- **Verifiable offline** — the hash of the rating decision is anchored in external public registers
- **Immutable after issuance** — neither party can unilaterally revoke or change an issued rating without generating a visible trace
- **Publicly auditable** — the complete decision path is available for review by authorized entities

## Dual-Brain Engine

The heart of the assessment system is the Dual-Brain Engine — a dual-track analysis architecture combining:

**Quantitative analytics** includes:
- Structure of governance and supervisory bodies
- History of changes in body composition
- Documentation of the decision-making process
- Regulatory compliance indicators

**Qualitative analytics** includes:
- Assessment of organizational culture based on internal documents
- Analysis of alignment between declared values and actions
- Verification of conflict of interest management procedures
- Evaluation of internal control system effectiveness

Both branches of analysis must independently demonstrate a sufficient level of governance quality for a rating to be issued.

## Hard Gates

Hard Gates are seven mandatory conditions whose non-compliance automatically excludes an entity from the rating process regardless of other assessment results:

1. No pending criminal proceedings against members of governing bodies
2. Regular and documented meetings of statutory bodies
3. No arrears to public bodies (Social Security, Tax Authority, National Court Register)
4. Current and publicly accessible register of beneficial owners
5. No conflict of interest in the composition of the supervisory body
6. Documented procedure for reporting irregularities (whistleblowing)
7. Consistency of legal and organizational structure with the declared governance model

Failure to meet any Hard Gate results in suspension of the rating process until the irregularity is remedied.

## Compliance Engine

Compliance Engine verifies an institution's compliance with applicable sectoral and EU-wide regulations. The engine is continuously updated in line with changes in EU and Polish law.

Compliance assessment includes:
- Sector-specific regulations applicable to the institution
- EU directives and regulations applicable in the given sector
- National implementation provisions
- Industry standards and best practice codes

## Break-Glass Protocol

Break-Glass Protocol is an emergency access procedure for rating records in crisis situations — entity liquidation, insolvency proceedings, criminal investigation by law enforcement authorities.

The Protocol defines:
- Who has access to records and under what circumstances
- How each access incident is documented
- What notification mechanisms are in place for assessed entities
- How data integrity is ensured in the disclosure process

## Certo Score and Certo Vector

Each issued rating consists of two elements:

**Certo Score** — a synthetic assessment of governance quality expressed on a numerical scale. It enables comparison of entities within a sector and over time.

**Certo Vector** — the direction of change in governance quality in the last assessment period. It takes five values: `++` (significant improvement), `+` (improvement), no change, `−` (deterioration), `−−` (significant deterioration).

The combination of Score and Vector provides a complete picture of both the current state of governance and its dynamics.
