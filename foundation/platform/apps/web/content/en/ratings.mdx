## How to read a Certo Rating

Each Certo Rating consists of three elements:

**Certo Score** — a synthetic assessment of governance quality on a scale from 0 to 100. A result above 70 indicates compliance with the minimum governance standards required by the Foundation.

**Certo Vector** — the direction of change in governance quality since the last assessment:
- `++` — significant improvement in governance quality
- `+` — improvement
- `→` — no material change
- `−` — deterioration
- `−−` — significant deterioration requiring action

**Validity date** — A Certo Rating is issued for a period of 12 months. After this period expires, the entity must undergo reassessment to maintain active status.

## Rating verification

Every active rating is publicly verifiable. To verify a rating:

1. Find the entity in the database below
2. Click the Trust Badge Certo next to the entity's name
3. The system will display the cryptographic proof of the rating along with the issuance date and scope of assessment

Verification requires no login or registration. It is available to anyone, at any time.

## Sectors and assessment criteria

### Public sector

Assessment includes: structure of governing bodies, transparency of the decision-making process, access to public information, public asset management, stakeholder servicing.

### Corporate sector

Assessment includes: composition and independence of the supervisory board, executive remuneration policy, conflict of interest management, relations with minority shareholders, ESG reporting.

### Medical sector

Assessment includes: structure of facility management, patient safety procedures, medical risk management, compliance with NHF and Ministry of Health regulations.

### Defense sector

Assessment includes: information security procedures, supply chain management, compliance with public contractor requirements, internal audit.

### Non-governmental sector

Assessment includes: financing transparency, structure of statutory bodies, fulfillment of organizational mission, donor relations management.
