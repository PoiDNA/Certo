## How to read a Certo Rating

Each Certo Rating consists of three elements:

**Certo Score** — a synthetic assessment of governance quality on a scale from 0 to 100. A result above 70 indicates compliance with the minimum governance standards required by the Foundation.

**Certo Vector** — the direction of change in governance quality since the last assessment:
- `++` — significant improvement in governance quality
- `+` — improvement
- `→` — no material change
- `−` — deterioration
- `−−` — significant deterioration requiring action

**Validity date** — A Certo Rating is issued for a period of 12 months. After this period expires, the entity must undergo a new assessment to maintain active status.

## Rating verification

Each active rating is publicly verifiable. To verify a rating:

1. Find the entity in the database below
2. Click on the Trust Badge Certo next to the entity's name
3. The system will display cryptographic proof of the rating along with the date of issue and scope of assessment

Verification requires no login or registration. It is available to anyone, at any time.

## Sectors and assessment criteria

### Public sector

Assessment covers: structure of governing bodies, transparency of decision-making processes, access to public information, management of public assets, stakeholder engagement.

### Corporate sector

Assessment covers: composition and independence of the supervisory board, executive remuneration policy, conflict of interest management, relations with minority shareholders, ESG reporting.

### Healthcare sector

Assessment covers: structure of healthcare facility governance, patient safety procedures, medical risk management, compliance with regulations of the National Health Fund and Ministry of Health.

### Defence sector

Assessment covers: information security procedures, supply chain management, compliance with requirements of public contracting, internal audit.

### Non-profit sector

Assessment covers: transparency of funding, structure of statutory bodies, implementation of the organisation's mission, management of donor relations.
