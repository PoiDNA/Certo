## How to read a Certo Rating

Each Certo Rating consists of three elements:

**Certo Score** — a synthetic assessment of governance quality on a scale from 0 to 100. A result above 70 indicates compliance with the minimum governance standards required by the Institute.

**Certo Vector** — the direction of change in governance quality since the last assessment:
- `++` — significant improvement in governance quality
- `+` — improvement
- `→` — no material change
- `−` — deterioration
- `−−` — significant deterioration requiring remedial action

**Validity date** — A Certo Rating is issued for a period of 12 months. After this period expires, the entity must undergo re-assessment to maintain active status.

## Rating verification

Each active rating is publicly verifiable. To verify a rating:

1. Find the entity in the database below
2. Click the Trust Badge Certo next to the entity's name
3. The system will display the cryptographic proof of the rating together with the date of issue and the scope of assessment

Verification requires no login or registration. It is available to anyone, at any time.

## Sectors and assessment criteria

### Public sector

Assessment covers: structure of governing bodies, transparency of decision-making process, access to public information, management of public assets, stakeholder engagement.

### Corporate sector

Assessment covers: composition and independence of the supervisory board, management remuneration policy, conflict of interest management, relations with minority shareholders, ESG reporting.

### Medical sector

Assessment covers: structure of facility management, patient safety procedures, medical risk management, compliance with NFZ and Ministry of Health regulations.

### Defence sector

Assessment covers: information security procedures, supply chain management, compliance with public contracting requirements, internal audit.

### Non-governmental sector

Assessment covers: financing transparency, structure of statutory bodies, fulfilment of organisational mission, management of donor relations.
