## How to read a Certo Rating

Each Certo Rating consists of three elements:

**Certo Score** — a synthetic assessment of governance quality on a scale from 0 to 100. A result above 70 indicates compliance with the minimum governance standards required by the Foundation.

**Certo Vector** — the direction of change in governance quality since the last assessment:
- `++` — significant improvement in governance quality
- `+` — improvement
- `→` — no material change
- `−` — deterioration
- `−−` — significant deterioration requiring action

**Validity date** — A Certo Rating is issued for a period of 12 months. After this period expires, the entity must undergo reassessment to maintain active status.

## Rating verification

Each active rating is publicly verifiable. To verify a rating:

1. Find the entity in the database below
2. Click the Trust Badge Certo next to the entity's name
3. The system will display cryptographic proof of the rating along with the date of issuance and scope of assessment

Verification requires no login or registration. It is available to anyone, at any time.

## Sectors and assessment criteria

### Public sector

Assessment covers: structure of governing bodies, transparency of decision-making processes, access to public information, management of public assets, stakeholder management.

### Corporate sector

Assessment covers: composition and independence of the supervisory board, management remuneration policy, management of conflicts of interest, relations with minority shareholders, ESG reporting.

### Medical sector

Assessment covers: structure of facility management, patient safety procedures, management of medical risk, compliance with National Health Fund and Ministry of Health regulations.

### Defense sector

Assessment covers: information security procedures, supply chain management, compliance with public contractor requirements, internal audit.

### Non-profit sector

Assessment covers: transparency of financing, structure of statutory bodies, fulfillment of organizational mission, management of donor relations.
