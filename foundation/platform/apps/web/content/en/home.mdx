## A Different Kind of Rating

Traditional governance assessments are opinions — texts produced by analysts that can be questioned, interpreted, and ignored. Certo Rating is something different: a cryptographic record of the rating decision process that exists independently of its creator's infrastructure.

Every rating decision is anchored outside its own infrastructure. It can be verified offline. It can be publicly audited. It cannot be unilaterally reversed or changed without generating a visible trace.

## Who Certo is For

Certo Rating is a tool for institutions that want to demonstrate — not merely declare — the quality of their governance:

**For public sector institutions** — rating as a tool of accountability to citizens and oversight bodies. Verifiable evidence of compliance with governance standards required by EU law.

**For corporations** — rating as a component of due diligence in M&A processes, fundraising, and investor relations. An objective basis for ESG reporting.

**For healthcare entities** — rating as confirmation of governance standards required by healthcare sector regulators.

**For defense organizations** — rating as verification of compliance with security procedures and public contractor requirements.

**For non-governmental organizations** — rating as a transparent signal to donors, partners, and beneficiaries.

## Trust Badge Certo

An entity holding an active Certo Rating may use the Trust Badge Certo — a cryptographically signed verification badge. Each click on the Trust Badge leads to a public registry confirming the current status and scope of the rating.

Trust Badge Certo is not a decoration. It is evidence — verifiable by anyone, at any time, without the intermediation of the Foundation.
