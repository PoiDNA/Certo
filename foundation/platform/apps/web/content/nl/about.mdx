## Missie

The Certo Governance Institute Foundation builds a culture of good governance through independent assessment and the promotion of governance standards in Poland and Europe. We operate at the intersection of law, technology and institutional ethics — where the decisions of governing bodies have real impact on citizens, employees and beneficiaries.

The Foundation's mission is to create a widely recognized standard for assessing the quality of governance, which will serve as a point of reference for public institutions, corporations, medical entities, defense organizations and the non-governmental sector.

## Onafhankelijkheid

The Foundation is independent from assessed entities, public authorities and commercial interests. This independence is guaranteed by:

- **Organisatiestructuur** — The Foundation operates as a non-profit entity with no capital ties to the technical operator Certo ID PSA
- **Financiering** — revenue from rating fees goes to a separate methodological fund
- **Procedures** — each rating goes through a multi-stage verification process with mandatory participation of external reviewers
- **Transparantie** — the rating methodology is publicly available and subject to periodic updates in accordance with EU guidelines

## Twee entiteiten van het ecosysteem

The Certo ecosystem consists of two separate legal entities:

**Certo Governance Institute Foundation** — an independent rating body based in Poland, owner of the Certo methodology, issues ratings and is responsible for their credibility.

**Certo ID PSA** — a commercial technology operator, maintains the IT infrastructure of the rating system, provides digital tools for assessed entities and their delegates.

This separation is a necessary condition for maintaining the independence of ratings — analogous to the separation of rating agencies from issuers of financial instruments.

## Evaluatiesectoren

Certo assesses institutions in five sectors:

1. **Publieke sector** — local government units, offices, state administration
2. **Bedrijfssektor** — commercial law companies, management boards, supervisory boards
3. **Medische sector** — hospitals, healthcare facilities, medical entities
4. **Defensiesektor** — defense industry entities, security system institutions
5. **Non-gouvernementale sector** — non-governmental organizations, associations, foundations

## Naleving van Europese normen

The Certo methodology is based on rigorous European and international standards:

- European Commission guidelines on Corporate Governance
- ISO 37000 standards (Governance of Organizations)
- EU Directive on non-financial reporting (CSRD)
- OECD recommendations on corporate supervision
